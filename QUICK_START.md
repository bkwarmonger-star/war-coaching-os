## Step 1: Login

1. Click the **"Login"** button in the top-right corner
2. Authenticate with your Manus account

## Step 2: Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard displays:
- **Active Clients:** Total number of clients you're training
- **Monthly Revenue:** Total income for the current month
- **Sessions This Month:** Count of scheduled training sessions
- **Pending Check-Ins:** Clients awaiting feedback on their submissions
- **Today's Schedule:** Upcoming sessions with client names and times
- **Recent Alerts:** New messages, check-ins, or consultation requests

---

## Step 3: Add Your First Client

1. Navigate to **Clients** in the top menu
2. Click **"Add New Client"**
3. Fill in the client profile:
   - Name, age, sex
   - Weight (lbs) and height (inches)
   - Fitness goals (strength, endurance, weight loss, etc.)
   - Any injuries or limitations
   - Current fitness level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
   - Training type preference (in-person, online, adaptive)
4. Click **"Save Client"**

---

## Step 4: Generate AI Workout Program

1. Go to **Programs** in the menu
2. Click **"Generate New Program"**
3. Select your client from the dropdown
4. The system automatically pulls their profile data
5. Click **"Generate Program"**
6. Wait for the AI to create a complete 12-week workout plan
7. Review the program (exercises, sets, reps, rest periods)
8. Click **"Save to Library"**
9. The program is now assigned to your client

---

## Step 5: Generate AI Meal Plan

1. Go to **Meals** in the menu
2. Click **"Generate New Meal Plan"**
3. Enter dietary information:
   - Dietary preferences (omnivore, vegetarian, vegan, keto, paleo, etc.)
   - Allergies (nuts, dairy, gluten, shellfish, soy, etc.)
   - Any dietary restrictions
   - Daily caloric output (based on activity level)
   - Client goals (weight loss, muscle gain, maintenance)
4. Click **"Generate Meal Plan"**
5. Review the 7-day meal plan with macros for each meal
6. Review the weekly grocery shopping list
7. Click **"Save Meal Plan"**
8. The plan is now assigned to your client

---

## Step 6: Track Client Progress

1. Go to **Progress** in the menu
2. Select your client
3. Log metrics:
   - **Weight:** Enter current weight in lbs
   - **Measurements:** Chest, waist, hips, arms, thighs, calves (in inches)
   - **Body Composition:** BMI, body fat %, hydration %, muscle mass, bone mass
   - **Photos:** Upload progress photos (same pose each time for comparison)
   - **Bloodwork:** Log bloodwork panels every 6 months
4. Click **"Save Metrics"**
5. View progress trends and charts over time

---

## Step 7: Review Client Check-Ins

1. Go to **Check-Ins** in the menu
2. View list of pending check-ins from clients
3. Click on a check-in to view:
   - Client's current weight
   - Energy level (1-10 scale)
   - Client's notes and observations
   - Photos uploaded
   - Recent measurements
4. Provide feedback:
   - Celebrate progress (even small wins!)
   - Suggest program adjustments
   - Give coaching notes and encouragement
5. Click **"Send Feedback"**

---

## Step 8: Manage Revenue & Payments

1. Go to **Revenue** in the menu
2. View:
   - **Monthly Revenue:** Total income for current month
   - **Income Goal Progress Ring:** Visual progress toward your goal
   - **Session Count:** Number of sessions this month
   - **Revenue by Service:** Breakdown by training type
   - **Payment History:** All transactions with dates and amounts

## Step 9: Set Up Your Services & Pricing

1. Go to **Services** in the menu
2. Click **"Add Service"**
3. Enter service details:
   - Service name (e.g., "1-on-1 Training Session", "Meal Plan", "Consultation")
   - Description
   - Service type
   - Price

4. Click **"Save Service"**
5. Your services are now displayed on your public profile

## Step 11: Manage Consultations

1. Go to **Consultations** in the menu
2. View all consultation requests from prospective clients
3. For each consultation:
   - Review client information
   - Check pre-consultation questionnaire responses
   - Update status (pending, confirmed, completed)
   - Process payment via Stripe

## Step 12: Message Your Clients

1. Go to **Messages** in the menu
2. Select a client to start a conversation
3. Send messages with coaching tips, program updates, or encouragement
4. Clients can reply directly in the app
5. Unread message count shows in the navigation badge

---

## Step 13: Track Leads & Referrals

1. Go to **Leads** in the menu
2. Add new leads:
   - Name, email, phone
   - Lead source (referral, social media, website, etc.)
   - Notes
3. Update lead status as they progress:
   - New → Contacted → Qualified → Converted (or Lost)
4. Track which sources bring the most conversions
5. View referral history to thank clients who refer others

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Can't login  
**Solution:** Make sure cookies are enabled in your browser. Try clearing cache and logging in again.

**Problem:** AI generators not working  
**Solution:** Make sure client profile is complete with all required fields. Check that your internet connection is stable.

**Problem:** Stripe payments not processing  
**Solution:** Make sure you've claimed your Stripe sandbox. Use test card 4242 4242 4242 4242 for testing.

**Problem:** Can't see clients in dropdown  
**Solution:** Make sure you've created at least one client first. Go to Clients → Add New Client.
